## Student State Management

Bethlemitas tracks students through multiple states including **Active**, **PIAR** (Individualized Educational Adjustment Plan), and **DUA** (Universal Learning Design). The platform provides comprehensive tools for psycho-orientators to monitor student progress and create detailed intervention plans.
